HOW TO TEST
-----------
- Browse tours and use the filters (date / duration / language) without logging in.
- Log in as a participant to book a tour: pick a date that matches the weekly
  schedule, add up to 3 extra people, give an email/phone for announcements.
  A booking can only be cancelled up to 24h before the start time.
- Log in as a guide to create/edit tours (locked once a tour has a booking),
  see reservations grouped by date, and file a post-tour report (number who
  attended + one photo) for dates that already happened. There are already two
  past dates with bookings to try this on (21 Jun and 25 Jun 2026).
- Log in as admin to see platform stats, guides with their languages,
  participants, and all bookings.
